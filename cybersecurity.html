The Hard Truth: Cellular Tracking

Important: Even with secure software, your location can be triangulated via cellular towers (IMSI catchers, Stingrays). However, using a secure OS prevents software-level spying on your messages, files, and browser history.

Stop carrying your phone if you need total physical anonymity. But for digital security, you MUST switch.

The Only Secure Operating Systems

There are only three operating systems that respect your freedom and security. Do not use anything else.

1. QUBES OS

For maximum compartmentalization.

  • Uses "Security by Compartmentalization" (Color-coded VMs).
  • If you open a virus in "Red", it cannot spread to "Blue" or "Work".
  • Dom0 (Admin) is totally isolated.
  • Best for: High-risk individuals, journalists, activists.

2. TAILS OS (The Amnesic Incognito Live System)

For anonymity and leaving no trace.

  • Runs from a USB stick. Leaves no trace on the host computer.
  • Forces ALL internet traffic through the Tor network.
  • Amnesic: It forgets everything when you shut it down.
  • Best for: Whistleblowing, accessing censored info, public computers.

3. GRAPHENE OS

The future of mobile privacy.

  • De-Googled Android. No Google Play Services. No spyware.
  • Hardened security kernel that protects against exploits.
  • Full control over your permissions. Apps cannot talk to each other without permission.
  • Works on Pixels (Google hardware with Graphene software is the ironclad combo).
  • Best for: Daily phone use without being tracked by Google/Apple.

3. Mullvad and Proton /h3>

Whenever you search something online, your Wifi router reports your search to your ISP (Internet service provider) and reports your IP address

  • Using a VPN prevents your location and IP adress from being seen
  • For a free VPN try Proton, while if you are looking for a higher security Mullvad VPN is a good option
  • Best for: Any user on a computer
